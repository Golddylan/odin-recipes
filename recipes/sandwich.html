Sandwich

Description

I don't really make sandwiches, but if I did, this would be on them.

Also, I love sandwiches, but I just prefer them from better sandwich makers.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Turkey
  • Avocado
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Add sliced avocado to one side of bread. Press down to keep in place.
  3. Add condiments as you please.
  4. Add Turkey.
  5. Enjoy.
