Tacos

Description

I could eat tacos forever.

Seriously, I don't know why, but I can.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Ground turkey or beef
  • Avocado
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Cook meat on oiled pan.
  2. Heat up shells or tortillas.
  3. Add meat to shells.
  4. Add cut avocado.
  5. Add hot sauce.
  6. Enjoy.
